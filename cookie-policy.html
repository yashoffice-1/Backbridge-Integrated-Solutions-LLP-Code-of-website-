Cookie Policy

Understand how BackBridge Integrated Solutions LLP uses cookies to enhance and personalize your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve site functionality, performance, and user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a. Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function (e.g., navigation, security).

b. Analytical/Performance Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our website (e.g., Google Analytics).

c. Functional Cookies

Remember user preferences such as language and display settings.

d. Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Enhance website performance and user experience
  • Analyze site traffic and usage trends
  • Personalize content and remember preferences
  • Improve marketing communications

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect certain functionalities or site experience. For more information, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party analytics and advertising tools (like Google Analytics) that place cookies on your device to collect usage statistics anonymously.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. The revised policy will be posted with a new effective date.
